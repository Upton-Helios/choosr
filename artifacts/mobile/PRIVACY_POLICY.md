# Privacy Policy for Choosr

**Effective date:** July 5, 2026

Choosr ("the App," "we," "us") is a decision-making app that helps you create lists of options and randomly pick one. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it's used, and your choices.

## Information We Collect

### Information you provide
The lists and options you create in Choosr (list names, choices, pick history) are stored **only on your device** using local device storage. This data is never transmitted to us or to any server we control, and we have no access to it.

### Purchase information
Choosr offers an optional one-time in-app purchase ("Premium") to unlock additional features. Purchases are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store and managed using RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management service. RevenueCat receives:
- An anonymous identifier generated for your device/app install
- Purchase and transaction data needed to verify and manage your purchase
- Basic technical data such as device locale, app version, and platform

We do not receive or store your payment details (card numbers, etc.) — these are handled entirely by Apple or Google. RevenueCat's own privacy practices are described at [www.revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).

### Information we do not collect
Choosr does not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, or camera data. The App does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs beyond the purchase-management functionality described above.

## How We Use Information
The limited data described above is used solely to:
- Operate the App's core functionality (managing your lists locally on your device)
- Verify and unlock purchased features
- Diagnose and fix technical problems with purchases, if you contact us for support

## Data Retention and Deletion
Your list data lives only on your device and is deleted automatically when you uninstall the App or clear its storage. Purchase records are retained by Apple, Google, and RevenueCat according to their own retention policies, which we do not control.

## Children's Privacy
Choosr is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Effective date" above.
